About

what does this service actually do?

Kgb DNS is a free dynamic DNS service that points subdomains of this domain name to your IP address, just like Duck DNS .

who are behind this service?

Well, it's just me. You can check out my website for other projects.

why are you doing this?

When I was a small kid, trying to learn the beauty of the baud, Duck DNS helped me a lot. So, I'm just doing my own favor to help others.

but, this looks identical to Duck DNS.

Exactly..

what data do you keep?

The service only stores minimal information to continue the whole functionality. So, it stores your email, token, domains and given ip addresses.

okay, but what happens to my data?

Your data stays in a private server and they will never be sold or processed for marketing purposes etc.

sounds about right but I don't trust you.

Well, okay. This service runs on a open-source software and you can just self-host your own solution. Check out the repository .
